Management Consultant

United Kingdom · £50,000 – £55,000 p.a. · 37.5 hrs/week

About the Role

We are seeking a qualified and experienced Management Consultant to support the analysis and improvement of our business operations. This is a genuine vacancy created to meet the company's operational needs and growth objectives. The successful candidate will be responsible for identifying inefficiencies, implementing practical solutions, and contributing to strategic development.

Key Responsibilities

  • ✓ Analyse internal business operations, systems, and processes to identify inefficiencies and areas for improvement
  • ✓ Develop and implement effective strategies to improve organisational performance and productivity
  • ✓ Conduct detailed data analysis to support evidence-based decision-making
  • ✓ Design and implement structured operational frameworks and process improvements
  • ✓ Prepare reports, business cases, and strategic recommendations for senior management
  • ✓ Support long-term business planning and organisational development initiatives
  • ✓ Ensure all activities comply with relevant UK laws, regulations, and professional standards
  • ✓ Collaborate with internal stakeholders to implement change management initiatives

Essential Requirements

  • ✓ Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • ✓ Minimum of 5 years' relevant professional experience in management consulting, business analysis, or similar roles
  • ✓ Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to interpret complex data
  • ✓ Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • ✓ Ability to manage projects independently and meet deadlines
  • ✓ Proficiency in Microsoft Office and standard business analysis tools
  • ✓ Clear understanding of organisational processes and operational improvement techniques

Who We Are

Denukan Network Limited UK is a premier financial consultancy and technology solutions provider, specializing in AI-driven financial services, core banking applications, and strategic business advisory. With our headquarters in the United Kingdom and a thriving branch in Nigeria that has been operational for over 10 years, we have established ourselves as one of the leading technology consultancy firms in the region.

Our expertise lies in integrating cutting-edge technology into everyday business operations, transforming how organizations leverage financial technology to create value, eliminate operational bottlenecks, and achieve sustainable growth.

We serve a diverse clientele including Microfinance Banks (MFBs), Microfinance Institutions (MFIs), FinTech companies, Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs), and corporations seeking to modernize their technological infrastructure and optimize their financial operations.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, top-class technological and financial services that meet the evolving needs of individuals, businesses, and organizations. We are committed to integrating tech-centered solutions into everyday life, transforming operational challenges into opportunities for growth and making this planet a better place to live through technology empowerment.

Our Vision

To build and empower clients with top-notch technological services while maintaining excellent service delivery standards. We envision becoming the foremost technology and financial consultancy partner across the United Kingdom, Nigeria, and beyond—recognized globally for our innovation, reliability, and transformative impact on businesses.

Our Journey

A decade of innovation, growth, and transformative impact

2014 - Foundation

2026 - Continued Growth

Operating across two continents with 500+ satisfied clients and continuing to innovate in financial technology solutions.
